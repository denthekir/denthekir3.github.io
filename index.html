Oxygen Reducing Masks

These masks not only look stylish, but also very effective. Having appeared relatively recently, they are becoming more and more popular among NBA stars and other professional players. During exercise, the mask blocks a certain amount of oxygen, which is why the body has to adapt to low oxygen in the blood during exercise. This helps to be more active and energetic during a real match, thereby achieving a better result. More info...

Cryotherapy

The human body is able to adapt to extremely low or high temperatures in order to survive. However, people are now voluntarily choosing to experience incredible discomfort in order to achieve better results. Temperatures can reach up to -222°F(-141°C) during this procedure. The result is pain relief after a long workout in the muscles, but this joy is possible only a couple of times a week, so as not to make yourself even worse. More info...

Cubic shaped basketball ball

Yes, the world has gone crazy, because not so long ago they invented a cube for basketball. Despite the fact that the idea at first seems really mindless, but in fact this thing helps professionals and just amateurs to train basket throws. The cube completely copies the weight and size of the ball so that the muscle memory remembers the characteristics of the cube and repeats the movement when thrown the ball. The odd shape helps players practice proper arm, shoulder, elbow, and even leg positioning. Modern problems require modern solutions. More info...
